Featured Research

LLMs for spear phishing detection

Graduate capstone (CYBR 560 · Hood College · Spring 2026), evaluating whether open-weight LLMs can support email threat detection in security operations environments.

Evaluated DeepSeek-R1, Llama 4 Scout, and Qwen 2.5-72B on a 205-email dataset of legitimate workplace emails, human-crafted phishing, and AI-generated phishing, a threat category absent from most existing benchmarks. Each model used a standardized zero-shot prompt with temperature set to zero for reproducibility.

All three models detected phishing with near-perfect accuracy. But they also flagged 43–76% of legitimate workplace emails as threats, misreading normal professional language as indicators of attack. The practical conclusion: these models work best as one detection layer within a broader system, not as standalone classifiers.
